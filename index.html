ABOUT ME

With eight years of experience in the gaming industry and educational technology, I’ve honed my craft as a 3D and technical artist. Presently, I’m immersing myself in the vast world of programming, eagerly expanding my skill set and sharpening my expertise.
My journey is one of exploration—absorbing as much knowledge as possible before carving out my own specialized niche and mastering it.

My career has taken me through a dynamic range of environments, from nimble startups where I thrived as a versatile multitasker, to large international companies where I collaborated in diverse, global squads. Each role has fueled my passion for problem-solving and creativity.

I thrive on unraveling the intricacies of logic, decoding how things tick, and find equal joy in transforming that understanding into something visually striking and elegantly designed.
